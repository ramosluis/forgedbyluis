I'm Luis, an engineer based in Mexico, with lots of hobbies, bouncing around from one to another being one of the main ones.

I started this blog because I like the idea of having my own place on the internet, however small it might be. It doesn't hurt that I enjoy writing too, I guess. I especially like writing about topics that I find interesting, whether it's some weird super specific subject I'm currently fascinated with which I'll drop in a few weeks, or some of my long-time favorite hobbies: reading, video games, 3D printing, D&D, computers and other expensive ways to distract myself from adulthood.

I mostly write book reviews, just to capture my feelings towards as I finish reading each one, I like going back and reading my own thoughts about books I don't remember much about, they sometimes make me feel like re-reading books. I like to stay honest to my own voice but I also like to try to be analytical of whatever I'm writing about. So a best of both worlds type of situation.

## What I read

Mostly it's Fantasy and Literary Fiction these days (it's mid-2026 as I'm writing this), but honestly I'm open to anything, a good story is a good story, regardless of "genre".
